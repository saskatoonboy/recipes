-Make rice 1 c of rice -cut firm tofu, mix with 1/4 c corn starch in a mix bowl, heat tbsp olive oil in skillet, add tofu, cook until golden. -add 2 garlic cloves, 1 tbsp ginger, 2 tbsp maple syrup, 2 tbsp soy sauce, 2 tbsp ketchup, 3 minced green onions, and 3/4 c vegetable broth (put in half the usual liquid), add 3/4 cup veggies such as bell pepper or broccoli or spinach. Cook until veggies are cooked through and broth has evaporated.
